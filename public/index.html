Login Using Mobile Phone

User can login to any websites/apps using his/her phone number without remembering any password. After user's consent, user profile can be shared with the website/app to customize the content for the user. The project is based on OpenID Connect protocal and was implemented using jQuery, Bootstrap, NodeJS and MongoDB.

A Smart IVR System

Indoor Localization

My research of indoor geolocation is divided into indoor geolocation using the more precise TOA-based localization for military and public safety applications and the RSS-based localization used in popular commercial applications for smart devices. The TOA-based localization was focused on the analysis of the effects of multipath on TOA-based indoor localization and discovey of algorithm to mitigate these effects.
Our research at CWINS, WPI

Body Area Network

My PhD dissertation was developed around body area networks with focus on channel measurement and modeling for wireless access and localization in and around the human body. The particular emphasis was on two applications, localization of wireless video capsule endoscope inside the small intestine and motion detection by video processing and RF signals transmitted from the sensors.
My research at CWINS, WPI
